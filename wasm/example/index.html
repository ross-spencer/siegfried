Signature testing environment: WASM

For testing DROID compatible signatures in the safety of your own browser.

Introduction

To allow you to test your new file format signatures Siegfried and its signature editing utility Roy has been compiled into this web-page. Load new signatures and test them against your own data before submitting them to The National Archves, UK.

WebAssembly (WASM) is a technology that enables you to run low-level code in the safety of your own browser, protecting your computer and your data.
